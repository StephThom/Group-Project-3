About Us

Unlike typical billiard parlors, Castle Billiards Lounge boasts 15 regulation size Brunswick Tables, a modern internet jukebox, high definition flat screen TVs which include our new jumbotron, and free WI-FI. Explore our full service bar complete with draft beer being pumped out at a cool 34 degrees Fahrenheit from our newly installed walk-in cooler.

We also boast a full menu kitchen that serves our ever popular Sizzlin' Platters and daily dinner specials proving we have more than just snack food. Along with these amenities, Castle Billiards Lounge is also very active with leagues and tournaments.

We hope you come by Castle Billiards Lounge to shoot some pool, have a bite to eat or enjoy a cold beer. It is our goal from the moment you step through the doors, that you feel welcomed as a member of the Castle family.
